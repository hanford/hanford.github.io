About Me

My name is Jack Hanford and I am not your average 19 year old. I grew up in Leland, a small town in Northern Michigan, while attending an even smaller school (14 kids in my graduating class.) I graduated in June 2012 one week later I was 2500 miles from home with no friends and no family.

I was in San Francisco, the technology capital of the world. My life was about to change faster then I think I could have ever predicted. I love to talk about my home town and probably don't have enough space to fill you in about it here but you can read about some of my adventures here or just get in touch with me to the right in the "Where to find me" section.
