Introduction

This tool can generate 10 unique graphs based on your facebook usage. You're able to visualize your activty based on hour, weekday, month and year. It also performs a sentiment analysis on each message you have ever sent so you're able to determine which friends make you happiest along with the ones that make you saddest. You can visualize what point in your (facebook) life that you have been happiest.

Happiness and saddness are calculated by the mood of each message you have participated in. On top of those graphs, this tool also maps your 40 most used words and the top 40 people you have participated in messages with.

Show me how!

Step 1

  1. First, head over to your facebook settings

  2. On the settings page click "Download a copy of your facebook data"

  3. Then Click the green "Start my Archive" Button.

  4. Then download the parser or clone it.


Step 2

By now you should have downloaded both the parser and your data from facebook
  1. Unzip the parser and then drag the the facebook-USERNAME.zip into the file you unzipped and Rename facebook-USERNAME.zip to 
    facebook.zip


Step 3

  1. Finally open up terminal and 
    cd
    into the parser then run 
    ./run-all.sh


That's it!

Be patient, sometimes chrome will ask if the page has stopped responding, simply click "wait".
